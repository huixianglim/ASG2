Slayers, a game where you can have fun

Bored and do not know what to do? Fear not! In this website you will be able to chillout and play with the simple game that we created. You can move around kill monsters and gain points. Compete against the world and earn you spot on the leader boards to get discounted price on our shop! In our game, we have 3 classes in total. The Knight, Ninja and Tank. Each with their own unique look and backstory. You can scroll down further to read more on it.

Class Ninja

A Sneaky like class

Deep into the mountains lie a rat who later became a human. Through its years of living in darkness, it has learnt to evolve into a being with stealth like powers and those powers converted through when he became human. The ninja class is one that is both sneaky and dangerous
